OUR STORY

Nectar began with a simple idea: make feeling good taste amazing. We set out to craft a drink that blends vibrant flavours with natural adaptogens and hemp extract — so you can sip something fun without the sugar crash. We’re a small crew of creators, taste-testers, and wellness nerds who believe in balance—between fizz and function, energy and ease. Every can of Nectar is made with intention, inspired by carefree summer days and a whole lot of colour.
